How to Measure

Waist: Measure at the narrowest part, usually just above the belly button.

Neck: Measure just below the larynx (Adam's apple), sloping slightly downward.

Hip (women): Measure at the widest part of the hips/buttocks.

About the US Navy Method

The US Navy body fat formula uses simple circumference measurements to estimate body fat percentage. While not as precise as DEXA scans or hydrostatic weighing, it's highly accessible and reasonably accurate for most people.

The formula uses the natural log of waist, neck (and hip for women), and height to calculate an estimated body fat percentage. It is used officially by the United States Navy for fitness assessments.
