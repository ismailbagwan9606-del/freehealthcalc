How Does the Age Calculator Work?

Our age calculator computes the exact difference between your date of birth and today (or any target date you choose). It breaks down your age into years, months, weeks, days, hours, minutes and even seconds.

You can also use the "Age At Date" field to find out how old you were (or will be) on any specific date in the past or future — perfect for calculating age at marriage, graduation, retirement, and more.
