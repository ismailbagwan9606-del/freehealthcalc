Who We Are

BMICalc.io is a free online health calculator platform designed to help people understand their body metrics quickly and accurately. We believe everyone deserves access to reliable health information — no subscriptions, no sign-ups, no paywalls.

All our calculators are built using internationally recognized formulas from the World Health Organization (WHO), the US Navy, and peer-reviewed medical research.

Accuracy & Disclaimer

All calculators on BMICalc.io use validated, peer-reviewed formulas. However, our tools are for informational and educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before making decisions about your health, diet, or exercise routine. Individual results may vary based on factors not captured by these formulas.

Privacy

We take your privacy seriously. All calculations happen directly in your browser — your personal health data (height, weight, measurements) is never sent to our servers or stored anywhere. Please read our Privacy Policy for full details.
