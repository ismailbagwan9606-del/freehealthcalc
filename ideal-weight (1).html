Why 4 Formulas?

How Is Ideal Weight Calculated?

Ideal body weight formulas were originally created for medical dosage calculations and have since been used in nutrition and fitness. Each formula uses height as the primary input, with slight adjustments for gender.

These are guidelines only — your ideal weight depends on many factors including muscle mass, bone density, and overall health. Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.
